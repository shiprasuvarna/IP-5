Roller Skating

Roller skating is traveling on surfaces with roller skates.
It is a recreational activity and a sport.
Roller rinks and skate parks are built for roller skating, though it also takes place on streets, sidewalks, and bike paths.
There are different types of skating like Ice Skating, Inline Skating, Roller Skating, Outdoor Skating.

Type Time
Ice Skating 30 mins
Inline Skating 2 hrs
Roller Skating 1 hr
Outdoor Skating 3 hrs
